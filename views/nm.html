WHAT IS Night Market?
Night Market is a cultural event held by TASA, Taiwanese American Student Association. Join other students for a night like none other, full of fun games, shocking performances, and not to mention FREE FOOD. It's the perfect opportunity to meet and mingle with other like-minded students, all the while learning more about Taiwan.

We welcome all those not allergic to FUN!
