Welcome!

No matter what it is about TASA that brought you to join us or check out our website, whether it's our famous annual night market, our events such as dumpling night, our general meetings, or even just a friend who is active in TASA, we encourage you to join us to continue to make TASA what it is.

We welcome anyone who wants to join, and we constantly strive to promote culture and diversity here at The University of Texas at Austin, while still being a friendly group of officers and junior officers!

Our Mission

The Taiwanese American Students Association at the University of Texas - Austin is a social / cultural / philanthropic organization dedicated to providing a sense of community for ethnic Taiwanese students, as well as anyone interested in Taiwanese culture.

We strive to create a sense of unity not only within the Taiwanese community here in Austin but also with students at other universities through the nationwide Intercollegiate Taiwanese American Students Association.

Our organization nurtures cultural awareness by building a community of motivated and dedicated students through our social, cultural, and volunteer activities that take place throughout the year.
